About NUSHA Hospital

Established in 2016, NUSHA Hospital is a fully accredited, not-for-profit healthcare organization dedicated to providing quality, affordable, and accessible medical services to the people of Wakiso District and surrounding communities.

We are committed to delivering patient-centered healthcare with compassion, professionalism, and integrity. Our services range from preventive and primary healthcare to diagnostic, maternity, emergency, and community outreach services, ensuring that individuals and families receive the care they need when they need it most.

Through community outreach programs, health education, medical camps, and partnerships with Village Health Teams (VHTs), we actively promote disease prevention, early diagnosis, and healthier communities.

At NUSHA Hospital, every patient is treated with dignity, respect, and compassion, regardless of their background. Our mission is to ensure proper healthcare service delivery to patients and improving the health of the community through setting standards that excel in health promotion, education and clinical care.
